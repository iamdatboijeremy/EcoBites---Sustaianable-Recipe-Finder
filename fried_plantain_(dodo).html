Fried Plantain (Dodo)

15 mins
144 cal per 100g

A simple yet delicious dish of sliced plantains fried until golden brown, often served as a side or snack.

Ingredients

  • 3 Ripe plantains
  • 1/4 cup Vegetable oil
  • To taste Salt

Instructions

  1. Peel and slice the plantains into rounds or diagonal pieces.
  2. Heat vegetable oil in a frying pan over medium heat.
  3. Fry the plantains in batches until golden brown and crispy, about 3-5 minutes per side.
  4. Drain excess oil on paper towels and sprinkle with salt.
  5. Serve immediately.

Your Notes

Why This Recipe is Sustainable

  • Plantains are grown in tropical regions with relatively low water requirements.
  • The dish uses minimal ingredients, reducing energy consumption and the need for long supply chains.
  • Plantains are often grown locally, supporting small-scale farmers.
